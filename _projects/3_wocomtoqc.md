The **Workshop on Computational Topology & Quantum Computing (WoComToQC)** is an interdisciplinary event bringing together researchers working at the intersection of:

- **Computational topology** — algorithms for topological invariants, persistent homology
- **Quantum computing** — quantum algorithms, quantum machine learning
- **Topological quantum computation** — anyons, topological error correction

### Role

Organizer and scientific committee member.

### Topics Covered

- Persistent homology and quantum algorithms
- Topological quantum error-correcting codes
- Applications of TDA in quantum information science
- Geometric methods in quantum machine learning
